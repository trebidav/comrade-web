COMRADE

Community Task Manager

A location-based platform for coordinating community tasks. Drop pins on the map, assign missions, chat in real-time, and build your local crew.

Try Demo View on GitHub

// Capabilities

SYSTEMS ONLINE

Map-Based Tasks

Pin tasks to real-world locations. See what needs doing nearby and claim missions within your operating radius.

Real-Time Chat

Coordinate with your crew through integrated messaging. Discuss tasks, share updates, and stay synced on the ground.

Gamification

Earn XP, level up, and unlock achievements. Track your stats and compete with fellow community members.

Team Ops

Add friends, form squads, and tackle tasks together. Built for communities that get things done as a unit.
